about me

Hey, I'm a 14-year-old web developer from Kocaeli, Türkiye.

I mainly work with JavaScript-based technologies like TypeScript and Next.js. I enjoy building modern websites and experimenting with new ideas. This website is something I made to improve myself, learn more, and share my projects, skills, and interests. It's also a way for me to practice my English while writing about what I do.

I've got a lot of free time, and most of it goes into either coding or just chilling with music. Music is a big part of my day — there's always something playing in the background no matter what I'm doing.

I also spend time playing games. I mostly play Minecraft, CS2, and Battlefield 1. There's no real pattern, I just play whatever I feel like at the moment.

At the end of the day, I just like building stuff, learning new things, and getting better little by little.
